Brand Guidelines

The visual language of the AI Responsibility Centre — logo, colour, type and UI. Use this as the basis for slides, documents and templates.

Logo

Primary lockup — on light backgrounds
Reversed lockup — on dark / photographic backgrounds
Badge / app icon — green tile, white arch
Mark only — for favicons & small uses

Keep clear space of at least the height of the arch around the logo. Don't recolour, stretch, rotate or add effects. The arch always reads as an open doorway.

arc-600 (#00805A) is the primary brand colour. arc-950 anchors dark sections; arc-50 is the soft tint for alternating bands. Ink tones carry all text.

Typography

Inter
AI in service of people.
Productive AI systems that put people at the centre.
Body copy uses Inter Regular at a comfortable measure. We pair bold, tightly-tracked headlines with calm, readable paragraphs.
Regular 400 Medium 500 Semibold 600 Bold 700 Extrabold 800

Typeface: Inter (Google Fonts, open source). Fallback: Helvetica / Arial. For Office templates, Arial or Aptos are acceptable substitutes.

UI elements

Buttons

Get in touch Learn more

Fully rounded (pill). Primary = arc-600; secondary = white with arc-200 border.

Tags & chips

safe transparent fair accountable

Soft arc-50 fill, arc-200 border, arc-800 text. Used for qualities, focus areas and event types.

Shape & feel

Generous rounded corners (cards ~24px, pills fully round), thin hairline borders (black @ 5–8% opacity), soft shadows, and alternating white / arc-50 bands. Clean, academic and confident — never loud.

Voice

"We don't just call for responsible AI — we engage."

Direct, evidence-led and human-centred. We build and test responsible AI rather than merely demanding it. Tone: precise, optimistic, grounded.
